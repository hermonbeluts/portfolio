About Me

Hello, my name is Hermon Beluts, and I’m an aspiring data scientist with a passion for artificial intelligence, survey research, and machine learning. I’m currently completing my B.S. in Information Science at Cornell University with a concentration in Data Science, where I focus on the intersection of data science and the social sciences.

I grew up in Aurora, Colorado, where my family immigrated from Eritrea. Before college, I had no background in data science or prior experience with coding, but I’ve always enjoyed math and problem-solving. That curiosity led me to explore Information Science, a field that has given me the flexibility to apply analytical and computational skills across diverse domains. I love that data can help us understand complex societal issues, and I aim to use it to drive meaningful research and innovation.

Outside of my academic work, I enjoy dance, journaling, cooking and baking, solving puzzles, trivia, movie reviews, and crocheting.

I’m open to exploring new opportunities in data science, AI research, and survey analysis. Explore my portfolio to learn more about my work!
